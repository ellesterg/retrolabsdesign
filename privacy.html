Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy applies to all apps published by Retro Labs Design, including but not limited to Starfield '84. It explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you. Our apps do not require an account, sign-in, or any personal details.

The apps store your wallpaper preferences (selected mode, color, brightness, etc.) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your phone.

Advertisements

Our apps may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous device information for ad personalisation in accordance with Google's privacy policies. This data is processed by Google, not by Retro Labs Design.

You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings, or remove ads entirely via the in-app upgrade.

For details on AdMob's data practices, see: https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

If you purchase "Remove Ads" or any other upgrade, payment is processed by Google Play Billing. Retro Labs Design never sees your payment details.

Permissions

Our apps may request the following Android permissions:

  • Internet — to load advertisements (only if ads are not removed)
  • Sensors — for optional gyroscope parallax (only if you enable it)

No other permissions are requested.

Third Parties

Our apps use these third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for advertisements
  • Google Play Billing — for in-app purchases

We do not share any data with any other third parties.

Children

Our apps do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The apps are rated for all ages and contain no user-generated content.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is nothing to access, modify, or delete. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored preferences from your device.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the new version will appear here. The "Last updated" date will reflect any changes.
